Plain-English explanation

The decision in simple terms.

We are not trying to time the market perfectly. Financial Services has been a meaningful contributor to your portfolio, but after the recent rally, the risk-reward has become less attractive. By reducing exposure from 18% to 12%, we protect part of the gains, reduce the impact of any sector-specific correction, and keep capital available for better opportunities. You still participate in the sector, but at a weight that better matches your risk profile.
