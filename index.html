As part of our ongoing portfolio monitoring, we have reduced your mid-cap exposure by 6% and moved the proceeds into short-duration debt.

The objective is to manage near-term volatility while preserving your long-term growth allocation. Mid-cap valuations have moved above our preferred comfort range, and given your upcoming liquidity need, we believe this is a prudent adjustment.

This does not change your broader investment strategy. It is a tactical action to balance growth, risk, and liquidity.
